About Us

Ninfriendos is your go-to band for a nostalgic, groove-packed journey into the world of video game music. Combining the best of funk and jazz, this Dublin-based group re-imagines beloved soundtracks from the 90s and 2000s with creative arrangements and infectious energy.

Our Story

The band formed through Dublin’s vibrant jazz jam sessions, evolving from a fusion project into a full celebration of gaming music. Highlights include sending off the iconic Token arcade bar in style, playing Fuinneamh Festival, and lighting up stages across Kilkenny and Dublin.

Where We Play

Whether it’s a themed gaming night, a wedding, or a festival, Ninfriendos brings the groove that gets hearts racing and feet moving.
